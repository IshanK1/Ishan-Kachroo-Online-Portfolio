Projects

Here are some projects I've worked on!

Scratch Project

This is the first project I worked on in CSP (Computer Science Principles). It is built using Scratch. For this project, my partner and I built an interactive story game. Players control Marco, a forgetful student that, unfortunately, didn't study for his test. Players have to go between 2 unique settings and a minigame in order to finish the story. I coded the minigame and a section of a setting. My partner and I had to thoroughly coordinate and communicate to get the different stages to fit together and run properly. Over the course of this project, I learned how to communicate my ideas better and think logically to solve continuity errors between the different stages.

MIT App Inventor Project

This project was made in MIT App Inventor. It is much larger than my earlier Scratch project, and the language was more challenging for me to learn and use. For this project, my partner and I made a variation of App inventor's "Quiz Me tutorial". We added our own questions as well as a home screen, score system, the ability to replay, and multiple choice questions. I worked on coding new questions, the score system, and part of the home screen. As the project grew larger, my partner and I learned the importance of properly planning out our work and staying commited to solving problems.
